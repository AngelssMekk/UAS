About me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Michael Angelsen, a passionate graphic designer from the University of Pelita Harapan, with a keen eye for creativity and a knack for bringing ideas to life. With almost 2 years of experience in the industry, I've had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects, spanning from branding and identity design to print collateral and digital media. My journey as a graphic designer has been marked by a commitment to excellence and a relentless pursuit of innovation. I believe in the power of design to captivate, inspire, and communicate, and I approach each project with enthusiasm and dedication.Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to collaborate with clients from various industries, helping them to achieve their goals through compelling visual solutions.

Whether it's crafting a memorable logo, designing engaging marketing materials, or conceptualizing a user-friendly website, I strive to deliver designs that resonate with audiences and make a lasting impact. Driven by a passion for design and a desire to push boundaries, I'm constantly exploring new techniques, trends, and technologies to enhance my craft. My goal is not only to meet my clients' expectations but to exceed them, delivering work that is both visually stunning and strategically effective. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I invite you to explore my work and discover the possibilities of visual storytelling. Let's collaborate and create something extraordinary together!
